Speech Engine Features

Sensory's TrulyHandsfree engine includes the following features (some are dependent on licensing level):
  • TrulyHandsfree™ Voice Control 4.0
  • Small footprint
  • Speaker-independent recognition (No training required)
  • User-defined trigger words for word spotting and speaker verification
  • Speech detection, ideal for noisy environments
  • Continuous or/and discrete speech recognition
  • N-best recognition results
  • Keyword activation
  • Word and phrase spotting
  • Speaker verification and speaker ID
  • Large Grammar-based vocabularies
  • Noise robustness
  • Context-switching capabilities
  • Foreign language support
  • Hardware independence
  • Operating-system independence
  • Easy portability to new platforms
  • Ideal for embedded, mobile, desktop and telephony applications
