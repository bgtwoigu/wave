Word-Spotting Grammars:

NOTE: Phrasespotting technology is now available and is the preferred solution over word-spotting.

If you need to recognize a word or phrase embedded in other speech (i.e., word-spotting), you can use the special word *any with a corresponding pronunciation .any as a wildcard to match the unspecified speech. Word-spotting behavior is controlled by the any parameter.

An example word-spotting grammar specification is as follows: 

      size       = small | medium | large;
  topping    = vegetarian | pepperoni | cheese;
  pizzaorder = (*sil%% | *any%%) ($size | $topping) [*sil%%] pizza (*sil%% | *any%%);
