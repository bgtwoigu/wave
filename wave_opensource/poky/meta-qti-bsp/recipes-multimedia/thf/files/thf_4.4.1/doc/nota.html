Out-of-Vocabulary Rejection

If you need to be able to detect when a complete utterance is not in your vocabulary, you can do so by adding the special word *nota with a corresponding pronunciation .nota to the grammar. If this special word *nota (stands for "none-of-the-above") scores higher than all the other words in the vocabulary, then we conclude that the utterance was indeed out-of-vocabulary. The out-of-vocabulary behavior is controlled by the nota parameter.

An example grammar specification including out-of-vocabulary (and wordspotting) is as follows: 

      size       = small | medium | large;
  topping    = vegetarian | pepperoni | cheese;
  pizza      = ($size | $topping) [*sil%%] pizza;
  pizzaorder = (*sil%% | *any%%) (*nota | $pizza) (*sil%% | *any%%);
